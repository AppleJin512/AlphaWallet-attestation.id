This website will be an Email Address attestor

This website will be an email address attestor, meaning that it issues cryptographically signed attestation to prove that an Ethereum key-holder owns the email address he claims.

A visitor to acquire such an attestation will need to provide his email address (signed), let us verify the email address by a typing verification code sent to their email address.

The flow (once complete) will look like this PDF preview.

Who is doing this?

Currently this website is operated by TokenScript team to demonstrate the use of attestation on the blockchain and for the web services that accepts attestations.

Is this reinventing the wheel?

In the past decades, there have been web-of-trust services to provide cryptographically signed messages to attest to user's email address, but there are 2 reasons we can't use them on the blockchain.

  • They do not accept secp256k1 the Elliptic Curve used in blockchains;
  • and they issue attestations without cryptographically hiding the email address being attested, making such attestations unsafe on the blockchain.

This service uses the algorithm which uses elliptic curve and zero-knowledge proof to provide privacy and security, so that the attestation can be used on the blockchain.

We hope to see more attestors using the new blockchain-safe algorithm.
